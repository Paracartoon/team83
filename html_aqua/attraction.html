BIG SUFT SHORE

Amidst the thrilling water slides and relaxing pools at Aquatica San Diego, some of California’s largest man-made waves swell at Big Surf Shores. Come float on waves as high as 5 feet at Aquatica’s largest wave pool. At 500,000 gallons of water, Big Surf Shores is also one of the largest wave pools in Southern California! Visit today for a swell time!
